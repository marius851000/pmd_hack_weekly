::: title 2 HackNews-eod "Explorers of Death is on hiatus"

*by FireFox6 on the Explorers of Death Discord server*

> Hello everyone. I'm sorry for the lack of updates on the romhack and am even more so that this is the type of news you're getting after so long. Unfortunately I have far too many real life commitments to be able to continue this project, and so either this romhack will be on hiatus for a very long time (best case scenario is a few months, but most likely years) or I will have to stop development entirely. I'm so sorry that I have to let you all down like this as we've built up an amazing community here, but I have no control over my situation. I know this isn't a good outcome but I hope you all can understand.
>
> Again, I am deeply sorry for this but I hope that what already exists of the romhack is satisfactory and that this community can remain as great as it is after this.
>
> Thank you for reading, and for being a part of this community. I hope this isn't a deal breaker for too many of you.

  <summary>v0.6 patch notes with spoiler</summary>

<blockquote>
<p><b>Features & Bug Fixes</b></p>

<ul>
  <li>Fixed a bug where starter selection could result in a crash on original hardware (credit: Irdkwia)</li>
  <li>Fixed the title card! (again)</li>
  <li>Various minor dialogue fixes</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Gameplay Adjustments</b></p>

<p>In general, Act 3 came out a little more difficult than intended. With these changes, we hope to ease the difficulty bump from Act 2 to Act 3, without removing the unique characteristics of the Act 3 dungeons that people seem to enjoy.</p>

<ul>
  <li>Kecleon Shop (Treasure Town):
    <ul><li>Lowered the purchase price of Reviver Seeds from 2000 to 1600</li></ul>
  </li>
</ul>

<p>The original intention of raising the price of reviver seeds was to discourage overreliance on retries while going through the game. While that price increase did result in this behavior, we think that the 2,000 Poke price point in v0.53 was a little too restrictive for the particular effect that we were looking for.</p>

<ul>
  <li>Horn Valley:<ul>
    <li>Increased the spawn rate of Gravelerocks</li>
    <li>Decreased the spawn rate of Poke</li>
  </ul></li>
</ul>

<p>Many players seem to have encountered trouble in close quarters encounters in Horn Valley, and as a result we placed higher emphasis on the primary universal tool that players should comfortably have access to during this dungeon.</p>

<ul>
  <li>Murky Forest:<ul>
    <li>Increased the spawn rate of Rainy Orbs</li>
    <li>Increased the spawn rate of Sandy Orbs</li>
    <li>Increased the spawn rate of Drought Orbs</li>
  </ul></li>
  <li>Glaceon:<ul><li>Lowered base HP</li></ul></li>
  <li>Castform:<ul><li>Removed Hail from movepool</li></ul></li>
  <li>Jynx:<ul><li>Secondary ability changed from Forewarn to Null</li></ul></li>
</ul>

<p>Your complaints about Murky Forest have been heard, loud and clear. A lot of the frustrating evasion-focused elements of the dungeon have been notably toned down - Jynx has lost access to evasion shenanigans completely, Glaceon now goes down much quicker, and changing the weather via orbs now has a much lower opportunity cost due to the higher spawn rate of these orbs, and the removal of Castform's ability to change the weather back to hail.</p>

</details>
